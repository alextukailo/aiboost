How it works

1

Build and manage
high quality datasets

At the heart of machine learning models that produce area are copious amounts of high-quality training data. However, amassing such data frequently involves large monetary investments, not to mention how time-consuming and complex it can get.

AiBoost allows you to accelerate the creation and management of high-accuracy training dataset. AiBoost's contains ready-made workflows, interfaces and tools for standard labeling activities. It also gives you access to AiBoost's top-notch labeling team. If you prefer to use your own labeling specialists or vendors, that is also an option at your disposal.

To ensure constant improvement, AiBoost learns from labels made by humans and provides option to use your pretrained model to automate and dramatically reduce labeling costs.

2

Build and collaborate using
AiBoost Studio Notebooks

Compute instance management in order to open, run or share a notebook takes far too long. AiBoost Studio Notebooks are Jupyter-based notebooks that you can bring up with a single click.

The underlying compute resources are fully elastic, allowing you to dial up or down the available resources effortlessly. Changes are carried out as background processes, allowing you to work without unnecessary pauses.

In addition to a streamlined editing workflow, AiBoost Studio allows you to share your notebooks with one click. Now your peers can benefit from the same information kept in a single location.

3

Automate the
MLOPS lifecycle

AiBoost Studio is your one-stop shop for ML development. The web-based visual interface provides total access, control, and visibility at each stage – from building models to deploying them.

Upload data, create notebooks, train and adjust models, navigate between steps to fine-tune your experiments, compare findings and deploy models to production - all in the same environment – then watch our performance skyrocket.

With AiBoost Studio’s common visual interface you can take care of all your ML-related activities: notebooks, experiment management, automatic model creation, debugging, model drift detection.

4

Deploy and control

With AiBoost all it takes to deploy your trained model into production is one click - and you can start generating predictions for real-time or batch data. A single click also deploys your model into a hosting provider of your choice.

All you need to do is choose the type of instance, including the maximum and minimum numbers you need. AiBoost will handle the rest – it launches the instances, deploys your model and sets up your application’s HTTPS destination.

AiBoost’s powerful Analytics tools allow you to identify and rectify any problems with accuracy in your models, such as when different sets of data are used to formulate predictions and train the models.
